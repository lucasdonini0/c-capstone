### What is this project?
This is my **first large project in C**, made to test my knowledge :)
The goal is simple: make money, unlock items, invest in crypto and stocks, gamble in minigames, and progress through days.

---

### How the game works
- You start with $0 and only the work option available.
- Working generates a random amount of money.
- By buying items in the shop, you unlock new mechanics:
  - Crypto
  - Stocks
  - Gambling (Coinflip and Keno)
- The game works in day cycles:
  - When you sleep, the day advances
  - Crypto and stock prices fluctuate
  - The game is automatically saved
